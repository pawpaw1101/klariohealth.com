Workflow

How it works

Four steps from scattered paperwork to a health timeline you can actually use.

1

Upload

Add a report by taking a photo, choosing an image, uploading a PDF, pasting text, or importing from email.

2

Parse

Klario extracts biomarker names, values, dates, units, reference ranges, and report source details.

3

Review

Confirm values, review flags, and keep ambiguous results from entering the record too quickly.

4

Track

Charts and timelines show how biomarkers move across tests, visits, medications, vaccines, and family profiles.
